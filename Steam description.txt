Rise of Nations is a mod lovingly made by the Hoi4 Community, for the Hoi4 Community. It will allow you to play 160 years of world history. Right now we have content packed WW1 & WW2, with the Interwar Era Content in the works.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/6OXWwEl.png[/img]
- WW1 Start Dates: 1910
- WW2 Start Dates: 1936
- Massive Technology Tree 1900-2060
- Massive Generic Focus Tree (1910-2060)
- Reworked Law System with much more immersion.
- Hundreds of Hand Crafted Realistic Portraits
- Integrated Expert AI
- Zombie Mode & Anti-Zombie Technology
- Hundreds of Unique Units with Custom Icons
- German, Italian and Japanese Puppet Decisions
- Hundreds of New Advisors and Companies
- Over 100 Formable Nations
- Hundreds of New Decisions and Events
- Hundreds of New Generals
- 60 WW2 Focus Trees

WORK IN PROGRESS AND PLANNED FEATURES
- 1900, 1914, 1933, 1939, 1940, 1941, 1942, 1943, 1944, 1945,1949, 1960, 1970, 1980, 1990 2000 & 2017 Start Dates.
- New Ideology System (Over 100 Subideologies)
- Space Tech and Space Race Decisions and Events
- Decolonization Decisions and Events
- CIA and KGB Decisions to Influence/Coup Countries
- Crime and Corruption Tab to deal with those issues.
- War on Drugs Events and Decisions, with Cartels having country tags.
- Terrorism Events and Decisions, with Terror Groups having country tags.
- League of Nations, United Nations and European Union events and decisions.
- Alien Mode
